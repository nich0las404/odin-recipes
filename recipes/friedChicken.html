Wingstop Fried Chicken

Wingstop's fried chicken is a masterpiece of culinary craftsmanship, delivering tender, juicy meat encased in an impeccably crispy, golden crust. Each piece is seasoned with a blend of spices that burst with flavor, creating an irresistible combination of savory and slightly spicy notes. The balance of moist, succulent chicken and the satisfying crunch of the exterior make for an unforgettable eating experience. Whether enjoyed solo or paired with Wingstop's famous dipping sauces, this fried chicken stands out as a pinnacle of comfort food excellence.

ingredients

  • 4 chicken drumsticks or thighs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

steps:

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk for at least 1 hour.
  2. Mix flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
  3. Coat marinated chicken in the flour mixture.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry chicken until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
